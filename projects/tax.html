Income Tax Calculator

Introduction

In this project, I am making a simple calculator to estimate our income taxes for 2017. The application can take the income inputs from users and outputs the income tax estimates according to the 2017 Federal and California State Income Tax brackets. Since I am a novice in finance, these calculations are from my personal knowledge and may not be accurate.
