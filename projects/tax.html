Income Tax Calculator

June, 2017

Introduction

Since the income of my wife and me fluctuates a lot during this time, we needed a tool to estimate our income tax since our income taxes. With my limited knowledge about how federal and state tax work, I wrote an application that can give us a quick solution. The application can take inputs of our annual income and deductions to calculate the federal and California state tax according to the tax brackets given by the Government. I used simple, yet efficient methods so that when the brackets are updated every year, I can update the problem quickly.
