Tax App

Introduction

In this project, I am making a simple calculator to estimate our income taxes for 2017. The application can take the income inputs from users and outputs the income tax according to the Federal 2016 Income Tax brackets. Since I am a amateur in tax, the result of this is only for reference. This application is available in web, Android and IOS.

Tools used:

  • HTML/CSS
  • JQuery Mobile
  • Adobe Phonegap
